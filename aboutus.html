Stay Liberated

Liberati Swimwear was founded in 2020 during the height of the Covid-19 pandemic by Shakur May. Born and raised in Buffalo, NY, Shakur came from a place where you either sink or swim. If you know anything about Buffalo, you know that it is cold 9 months out of the year. So, when the sun comes out, you better be ready to show out. Shakur wanted to create a brand that would allow people to express themselves through fashion. He wanted to create a brand that would allow people to feel confident in their own skin. He wanted to create a brand that would allow people to feel liberated. Thus, Liberati Swimwear was born.
